7 Travel Essentials Everyone Needs This Summer

As the summer heat dawns on us, travel season is right around the corner. Get your swimming trunks and sunshades ready while we bask in the sun and heave a breath of fresh air from all of our responsibilities. Don’t forget these seven things while packing for your trips, no matter where you’ll be going!

1. La Roche-Posay Anthelios Invisible Fluid Facial Sunscreen SPF 50+

This hot-girl summer, we will not be getting any sunburns! Don’t forget to grab one of the best mineral sunscreens on the market.

Review: “I just recently got back from Ilocos. The whole time as my sun protection and it did not disappoint. Since this waterproof, especially when under the hot unforgiving sun and the ocean my skin was still protected and no white cast.” - Lina B.

2. Islander Flip-Flops

Walking on bare feet does feel uncomfortable. Make sure your grippers are well-protected with quality slippers.

Review: “I love wearing flip flops because they are fresh and I feel freer wearing them. And for me there's only Islander flip flops. Although most people would probably love to have the most popular brand nowadays, the Havaianas, I would prefer the local brand here in the Philippines, Islander flipflops.” - @darbybelle

3. Anti Hair Fall Shampoo Bar

Gorgeous girls go sustainable! That’s why we advocate for natural shampoos in sustainable packaging, just like Sibol’s revolutionary shampoo bar.

Review: “​Bought these shampoo bars for convenience: easy to pack, lightweight, and lasts up to 60 washes. They're effective and eco-friendly. I brought these shampoos with me when I moved to Canada and I just wished I have hoarded them because it's not available here. So I always ask my relatives to bring me these whenever they're from PH.” - Jamayne B.

4. Luggage Strap Jacket Gripper

With the amount of luggage we carry, our hands become exhausted quickly. This simple luggage strap solves all our problems for that.

“Used this on an international trip from the east coast of USA to Australia. Used in 6 connecting flights and hours of layovers in 6 airports. They still look brand NEW! Purse clipped to the carry on. Carry on clipped to my larger luggage so they can roll together. It worked great!” - 3aruin

5. Fitlab Beach Towel

Lying down on gravel and sand isn’t the most comfortable experience. Every traveler should have a beach towel of great quality.

Review: “I was pleasantly surprised with its many uses. I usually just like to lie down on the beach and not bring a lot of things since I tend to fall asleep. I love that I can use this as a beach mat and I LOVE the zippered compartment on the upper right side of this towel. It can fit my phone and a small pouch for a little cash. It can fold really well too so. It's great to bring on trips as a towel. I bought two more of these as gifts to my beach-loving friends and they are just as in love with this as I am.” - Lisha C.

6. Aquaflask

The summer heat can get relentless sometimes. It’s best to come prepared with a water bottle to keep you cool and hydrated.

Review: “AquaFlask, you are one of the best investments I've made this year! I've only used it twice but both times the minimal ice (as in 6 cubes) has still been strong 13 hours later and my cold beverage has stayed cold. I put pre-chilled tea in it in the morning, and drink it throughout the day.” - Lizard S. Leaping

7. Apple AirPods 3rd Generation

Traveling is always better with a soundtrack! Get lost in the music with compact, travel-friendly earbuds such as Apple’s AirPods.

Review: “I got these for my brother as a gift for passing his driver’s permit test and he loves them. They have lasted forever with him and he uses them hourly every day not just daily. These are amazing and well worth the price and make for a great present that will surely brighten up the day of anyone you give them to. They are also very great for working out he loves to use them at the gym to listen to music or watch movies and block out the annoying gym music.” - Webblz
